Meridian AI is a Seattle-based B2B SaaS startup (~200 employees, ~$85M raised through Series C, led by Cascade Ventures with participation from Ironwood Growth and Northgate Capital) that builds an AI-powered developer productivity platform called Meridian Studio. The platform helps engineering teams automate code review, generate and maintain technical documentation, surface technical debt, and track code quality trends over time — positioned as "the operating system for engineering health." Founded in 2021 by former Stripe infrastructure engineers and a Datadog product leader, the company has an engineering-led culture that prizes getting things right over moving fast: weekly deploys are the norm but cutting corners is not. The team is async-first, low-ego, and largely in-office two days a week out of their Pioneer Square office. The engineering org (~40 engineers) is organized into three areas: Platform (core data pipeline, API, and infrastructure), Integrations (IDE plugins, Git provider connectors, CI/CD hooks), and AI Features (the LLM-powered analysis and suggestion layer). Product has 8 PMs. Tech stack: Python backend services, TypeScript/React frontend, PostgreSQL (primary datastore), Redis (caching and job queues), Kafka (event streaming from Git webhooks and CI signals), AWS infrastructure running on EKS with Lambda for event-driven workloads and S3 for artifact storage. AI features use Claude and GPT-4o depending on the task — internal tooling evaluates both continuously. Hiring bar is high: the process runs roughly 30 days and includes a take-home technical project (scoped to 3-4 hours), a system design interview, and a team culture conversation. Competitors include Codacy, Sourcegraph, and LinearB. Meridian differentiates on depth of AI integration and the breadth of signals it ingests (PRs, CI runs, deployment frequency, incident correlation) rather than point solutions for single problems.
