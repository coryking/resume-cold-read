## Description

Meridian AI's Platform Engineering team owns the infrastructure that powers everything customers see: the data ingestion pipelines that process millions of Git events per day, the APIs that IDEs and CI systems talk to, the storage layer that makes code history queryable at scale, and the background workers that drive our AI analysis features. We're looking for a Senior Software Engineer who wants to work on hard distributed systems problems with real customer impact.

### What You'll Do

- Design and build reliable, high-throughput backend services and APIs consumed by internal teams and external integrations
- Own data pipeline work end-to-end: schema design, ingestion, transformation, and surfacing queryable results to product features
- Collaborate closely with the AI Features team to build infrastructure that makes LLM-powered analysis fast, cheap, and observable
- Lead technical design discussions, write RFCs, and drive alignment across engineering subteams
- Mentor junior and mid-level engineers through code review, pairing, and design conversations
- Participate in on-call rotation; own the reliability of what you ship

### What We're Looking For

- 5+ years of backend software engineering experience, with meaningful time in production distributed systems
- Strong Python and/or TypeScript skills; comfort switching between them
- Deep experience with PostgreSQL: schema design, query optimization, migrations at scale
- AWS fluency — EKS, Lambda, S3, and the surrounding ecosystem
- Demonstrated ability to design systems from first principles and communicate tradeoffs clearly
- Experience owning a service end-to-end: you've been woken up by your own alerts

### Nice to Have

- Experience building or operating ML inference infrastructure or LLM-serving pipelines
- Kafka or other event streaming systems (schema design, consumer groups, backpressure handling)
- Kubernetes operations experience beyond "it runs in a cluster"
- Background in developer tools, observability platforms, or code analysis systems

### Why This Role

This isn't a maintenance job. The platform is growing faster than the team, which means there is real design work to do — not just feature tickets. You'll have ownership over architecture decisions, a direct line to the product team, and colleagues who will push back on your designs in the best possible way.

Meridian offers competitive base compensation, meaningful equity, fully-covered medical/dental/vision, 401k with 4% match, $2,000/year learning budget, and 20 days PTO plus company holidays.
